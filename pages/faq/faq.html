Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about selling your house for cash

General Questions

How does the cash buying process work? +
It's simple: 1) You submit your property information through our online form. 2) We evaluate your property and make you a fair cash offer within 24 hours. 3) If you accept, we schedule a closing date that works for you and handle all the paperwork. You can close in as little as 7 days and receive cash payment.
Do I have to pay any fees or commissions? +
No! Unlike traditional real estate sales, there are zero fees, no realtor commissions (typically 6%), and no closing costs. We cover all of these expenses. The cash offer we make is the amount you receive.
What condition does my house need to be in? +
We buy houses in ANY condition - from pristine to properties needing major repairs. You don't need to clean, repair, paint, or stage anything. We buy houses as-is, which means you save money and time on repairs and maintenance.
How quickly can I get my cash offer? +
We typically provide cash offers within 24 hours of receiving your property information. In urgent situations, we can often provide an offer even faster - sometimes within a few hours.
Is the cash offer obligation-free? +
Absolutely! Our cash offers are 100% no-obligation. You can take time to review the offer, compare it with other options, and make the decision that's best for you. There's no pressure to accept.

Timeline & Process

How fast can I close? +
We can close in as little as 7 days if you need to sell quickly. However, we're flexible and can work with your timeline. If you need 30, 60, or even 90 days, we can accommodate that too. You choose the closing date that works best for you.
What happens after I submit my information? +
After you submit your property details, our team will review the information and may reach out with a few follow-up questions. We'll then evaluate your property and present you with a fair cash offer, typically within 24 hours. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately after submitting.
Do I need to be present at closing? +
In most cases, yes, you'll need to sign the closing documents. However, if you're unable to attend in person, we can arrange for remote signing or use a power of attorney in certain situations. We're flexible and will work with your circumstances.

Property & Pricing

How do you determine the cash offer amount? +
We base our offers on several factors: current market value, property condition, location, recent comparable sales in your area, and necessary repairs. We strive to make fair, competitive offers that reflect the true value of your property while considering the convenience and speed we provide.
What types of properties do you buy? +
We buy all types of residential properties including: single-family homes, multi-family properties, condos, townhouses, mobile homes, and even land in some cases. Regardless of size, age, or condition, we're interested in making you an offer.
Will your offer be lower than market value? +
Our cash offers reflect the convenience and speed we provide. While we make competitive offers, they may be slightly below retail market value because we buy as-is (saving you repair costs), pay all closing costs, and eliminate agent commissions (typically 6%). When you add up these savings, many sellers find our offers very fair.
Can I sell if I'm behind on payments or facing foreclosure? +
Yes! We specialize in helping homeowners in difficult situations. If you're behind on mortgage payments or facing foreclosure, we can often move quickly enough to help you avoid foreclosure and protect your credit. Time is critical in these situations, so contact us immediately.

Special Situations

Can I sell an inherited property? +
Absolutely! We frequently work with heirs who have inherited properties they don't want or can't maintain. We can help you navigate the probate process if needed and make the sale as smooth as possible.
What if my house has tenants? +
Not a problem! We buy tenant-occupied properties regularly. We can work with existing leases and tenants, so you don't need to worry about evictions or waiting for leases to end.
Can I sell if I owe more than the house is worth? +
This situation (being "underwater") requires special handling. While we can't always help in every case, we can often work with your lender to negotiate a short sale. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.
What if I need to remove personal belongings first? +
You can remove whatever you'd like before closing. However, if you need to leave items behind, we can handle the cleanout for you at no extra charge. This is especially helpful if you're relocating quickly or dealing with an estate.

Trust & Legitimacy

Are you a legitimate company? +
Yes! We are a licensed, insured, and professional home buying company. We've purchased hundreds of properties and have an excellent reputation. You can verify our credentials, check our reviews, and we're always happy to provide references from past sellers.
How do I know this isn't a scam? +
We understand your concern. Legitimate indicators: We never ask for upfront fees, all closings are handled by licensed title companies or attorneys, you can verify our business licenses, we have real reviews from real customers, and we never pressure you to sign anything. Always work with a real estate attorney if you have concerns.
Should I consult with an attorney? +
While not required, it's always a good idea to have an attorney review any real estate transaction, especially if it's your first time selling or if you have concerns. We welcome attorney involvement and will work with your legal counsel to ensure you're comfortable with every aspect of the sale.
